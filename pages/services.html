Individual Therapy Sessions

Our one-on-one therapy sessions provide personalized care tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or simply seeking personal growth, our licensed therapists are here to support you in a confidential and non-judgmental environment.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors to improve emotional regulation and develop personal coping strategies.

Book Session

Psychodynamic Therapy

Explores unconscious patterns of behavior and past experiences to gain insight into current challenges and emotional difficulties.

Book Session

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Combines traditional therapy with mindfulness practices to help you become more present and develop healthier responses to stress.

Group Sessions

Join our supportive group therapy sessions led by experienced therapists. Connect with others, share experiences, and build a sense of community as you work together toward well-being.

Addiction

We know addiction isn’t just about substances it’s about pain, patterns, and the parts of us that are trying to cope. Between the two of us, we’ve worked closely with people navigating alcohol use, substance dependency, relapses, and the deep emotional weight that comes with all of it. This group is a space to slow down, be seen, and talk about what’s really going on withou t shame, without pressure. No one here will tell you to “just stop.” We meet you where you are, whether you’re starting out, starting over, or just tired of doing this alone. You don’t have to have the right words. You don’t have to be “ready.” You just have to show up. We’ll take it from there, together.

Emotional Wellness Circle

Burnout isn’t just about being overworked it’s when your mind feels foggy, your body feels heavy, and even rest doesn’t feel like it’s enough. In this group, we take a pause. We talk about the stress, the guilt, the feeling of always having to keep going and what it means to care for yourself when you're already running on empty. We’ve held space for students, professionals, caregivers, and others who feel stretched thin. This is a soft place to land. No pressure. No judgment. Just support, reflection, and small steps toward feeling human again.

Join Group

Self-Esteem Building Group

You don’t have to figure everything out on your own. This is a space for women who are feeling stretched thin by expectations, by roles, by the constant need to “keep it together. We’re creating a gentle space for women to pause, reflect, and connect. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, unheard, or just in need of a space that feels safe and supportive this group is for you. Led by two women therapists, this space will be about sharing stories, holding space for each other, and slowly coming back to yourself. No pressure to be “strong” or “sorted.” Just space to be real
